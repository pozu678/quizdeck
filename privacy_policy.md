# Privacy Policy — QuizDeck

**Last updated:** April 2026

## 1. Introduction

QuizDeck ("the App") is developed by Apozu (hereinafter "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

By using QuizDeck, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide
- **Account data:** When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address.
- **User-generated content:** Flashcard decks and questions you create within the App.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically
- **Usage data:** Sessions completed, study scores (K-Score), and study streaks. This data is stored to personalize your experience.
- **Device information:** Basic device identifiers used by Google AdMob to serve ads.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's features (deck creation, study sessions, duels).
- Save your progress and synchronize data across devices (Premium users).
- Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob (free users).
- Improve the App based on usage patterns.

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

QuizDeck uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google Sign-In | Authentication | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Firebase / Firestore | Data storage (Premium) | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |
| Google AdMob | Advertising (Free users) | https://policies.google.com/privacy |

---

## 5. Data Storage

- **Free users:** Your decks are stored locally on your device using Hive (offline storage). No deck content is sent to our servers.
- **Premium users:** Your decks are synchronized and stored on Firebase Firestore servers managed by Google.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings menu inside the App.
- Upon account deletion, all your data stored on our servers is permanently removed within 30 days.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

QuizDeck is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

---

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
